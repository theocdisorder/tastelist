My TV Taste Profile

A taste that spans the full prestige-drama canon alongside some of the sharpest comedy ever made for television — and takes both equally seriously.

The drama selections (Breaking Bad, The Wire, Succession, Game of Thrones, Friday Night Lights) share a preoccupation with power, moral collapse, and institutions failing their people. The comedy picks (30 Rock, Veep, Arrested Development, Seinfeld, Community) are all writer-driven, densely layered, and reward rewatching.

The common thread across both: intelligence on the page. Whether it's Vince Gilligan's plotting or Armando Iannucci's political satire, the writing always comes first.
